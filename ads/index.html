Email Images

Measured in pixels. Displays in email clients.

Full-width background image

As large as possible while maintaining small file size, however usually no bigger than 1920x1080 is needed.

All other images

16:9 or 1:1 aspect ratio when possible. Dimensions should be @2x the largest width they will display.
